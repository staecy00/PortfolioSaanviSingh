01. FONT BOOKLET

This booklet explores Baskerville, a transitional serif typeface designed by John Baskerville in the 18th century. Known for its refined elegance, sharp contrast, and balanced proportions. Baskerville bridges the gap between old-style and modern typefaces. Through this project, I aimed to study its anatomy, visual harmony, and timeless appeal while experimenting with layouts that highlight i ts sophistication and readability.
